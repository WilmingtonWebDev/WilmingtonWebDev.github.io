At the moment Wilmington Web Devs is just Tom Marks and Matt Eckman who both work out of TheMillSpace in central Wilmington.

Tom Marks

Tom spends most of his time writing Python or defining build pipelines in Jenkins and trying to automate all the things for MediaGamma a London based data science startup. He started off building websites, writing Javascript, and wrangling css first for a startup called Metric and then building production tools for The Moving Picture Company.

Reach out to him via @ThomasMarks

Matt Eckman

Matt Eckman has worked as a Database Analyst, Front-end Web Developer, and PHP Developer. Most of his experience is in designing and building applications with PHP and Javascript, and he is as skilled in doing high-level architectural work as in building the bones of an application. He is a Co-Founder of Kurbi, a health-tech startup focused on connecting the social and clinical support people living with chronic disease need to effectively self-manage their health. Matt runs the PHP developer meetup group at Start It Up Delaware. - StartItUpDE
